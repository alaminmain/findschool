# ASO Keyword Research — Find School BD

Play Store does not have a separate keyword field (unlike iOS). Keywords must
be woven into the **title**, **short description**, and **full description**.
Relative ranking matters: title > short desc > full desc.

## Primary terms (must appear in title or short desc)
| Term                 | Lang | Intent                               | Notes                          |
|----------------------|------|--------------------------------------|--------------------------------|
| find school          | en   | Brand match                          | App name anchor                |
| school bangladesh    | en   | Broad search                         | In title or short desc         |
| স্কুল                | bn   | Core term                            | Must be in bn listing          |
| স্কুল খুঁজুন         | bn   | Transactional                        | In title                       |
| EIIN                 | both | Exact ID lookup (high conversion)    | Hardcoded identifier           |
| IPEMIS               | both | Domain trust signal                  | In full desc                   |
| BANBEIS              | both | Domain trust signal                  | In full desc                   |
| offline              | en   | Differentiator                       | In short desc                  |
| directory            | en   | Category                             | In title tail                  |

## Secondary terms (weave into full desc, do NOT stuff)
- school finder, school locator, school map bd
- primary school bd, secondary school bangladesh
- dpe, education ministry bangladesh
- bn: প্রাথমিক বিদ্যালয়, মাধ্যমিক বিদ্যালয়, বিদ্যালয় তালিকা, উপজেলা, জেলা

## Long-tail (blog / future feature hooks — NOT for listing)
- EIIN number lookup, find school by EIIN
- school near me bangladesh offline
- government school list bangladesh

## Negative keywords (avoid)
- "admission" — different user intent, would miscue reviews.
- "result" / "exam" — drives wrong traffic to a directory app.
- "fee" — we don't list fees; review bombs likely.

## Title + short-desc exhaustion check (en)
Title: **Find School BD — Offline Directory** → covers `find school`, `bd`,
`offline`, `directory`.
Short: **65,000+ schools in Bangladesh. Search & navigate — works fully offline.**
→ adds `schools`, `bangladesh`, `search`, `navigate`, `offline` again for density.

## Competitor sanity check (manual, before launch)
Search these on Play Store and note the top 3 install counts + last-update dates:
- `school bangladesh`
- `EIIN`
- `স্কুল খুঁজুন`
- `ipemis`

If a competitor has >100k installs and an update within 90 days, plan a
point-of-difference hook (we expect ours to be "truly offline" + "no ads").
