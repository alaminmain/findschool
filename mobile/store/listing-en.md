## Full description (max 4,000 chars)

Find School is the fastest way to locate any primary or secondary school in
Bangladesh — even when you have no internet.

The entire national school directory is built into the app. Search 65,000+
schools by name, EIIN, upazila, or district. Tap any result to see the
address, school level, and a precise map marker. One tap opens turn-by-turn
directions in Google Maps.

★ FULLY OFFLINE — Browse the full directory with zero mobile data.
★ BLAZING SEARCH — Typo-tolerant FTS5 search returns results in milliseconds.
★ ACCURATE LOCATIONS — GPS coordinates sourced from IPEMIS and verified
  against public records.
★ GET DIRECTIONS — Open any school in Google Maps with one tap.
★ NO ACCOUNTS, NO ADS, NO TRACKING — Your searches stay on your device.

WHO IS IT FOR?
• Parents comparing schools in their area
• Education officers visiting schools in the field
• Journalists and researchers working with school-level data
• NGO staff running rural programs
• Students looking up a specific EIIN

HOW IT WORKS
The app ships with a compiled SQLite database of every government-registered
primary and secondary school in Bangladesh. On first launch we copy the
database to your device — after that, nothing leaves your phone. Updates
arrive with normal app updates; no background data use.

DATA SOURCES
Data is compiled from IPEMIS (Directorate of Primary Education) and BANBEIS
(Bangladesh Bureau of Educational Information and Statistics). We do not
claim affiliation with either body.

MISSING A SCHOOL?
Email hello@findschool.app with the EIIN and we'll add it in the next
release.
